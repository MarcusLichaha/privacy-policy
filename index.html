Privacy Policy for ZedList

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Location data: GPS coordinates to determine your country
  • Usage data: How you interact with our app
  • Device information: Type of device and browser used

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide location-specific buying and selling services
  • To display your country flag to other users
  • To enable communication between buyers and sellers
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send important service updates

3. Information Sharing

  • Your phone number is visible to users interested in your listings
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Information may be shared with law enforcement if required by law

4. Location Data

  • We use GPS to determine your country for service functionality
  • Location permission is required to access the app
  • Your exact location is not stored or shared, only your country
  • You can change your viewing country but cannot post in foreign countries

5. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Your information is stored securely on our servers
  • However, no internet transmission is 100% secure

6. Your Rights

  • You can access and update your personal information
  • You can request deletion of your account and data
  • You can withdraw location permission (but this limits app functionality)
  • You can contact us with privacy concerns

7. Cookies and Tracking

  • We use local storage to remember your preferences
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

8. Children's Privacy

  • Our service is not intended for users under 18
  • We do not knowingly collect information from minors

9. International Transfers

  • Your data may be processed in countries outside your own
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place

10. Contact Us

  • For privacy questions or concerns, contact us through the app
  • To request data deletion, use the account settings

11. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy from time to time
  • Material changes will be communicated to users

This policy is effective as of January 2025.
